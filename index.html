Skiing in the United States


At the time of making the following maps there were 363 ski resorts across the USA. When deciding where to ski a lot of factors are taken into consideration, some of those considerations are geographically visualised in the following maps. The first resort to open was in 1924 at Mountiain High California, however, the first chair lift didn't open until 1936 in Sun Valley Idaho. The largest ski resort in the USA which also has the most lifts is Park City in Utah at 7300 acres and 41 lifts. Telluride as the most vertical at a ski resort in the USA.
